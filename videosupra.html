Supra SAEIndia

SUPRA SAEINDIA Student Formula is a national level engineering competition designed to develop and provide a platform for student engineers to experience, build and learn. Students are given a unique opportunity to test their theoretical knowledge in a practical context.

"Team Malaviya Racing" represented our college in SUPRA SAEINDIA 2019. The competition was organized at Buddha International Circuit, Greater Noida. "Team Malaviya Racing" bagged 36th rank among 130 colleges that participated in SUPRA SAEINDIA 2019.

Ecokart

Ecokart series is a national event for students pursuing engineering. The competition is to design and fabricate a single passenger kart which is operated via battery.

Team "Ecolution 3.0" represented the university in Ecokart 2019 and cleared the Virtuals with flying colors with an AIR of 12 and cleared the Dynamics Round with an AIR of 9.

Members of the team work hard and all efforts are made with the motive to get the exposure of the automobile engineering profession. The members combine their specialized abilities for advanced research, development, design, manufacturing and utilization of vehicles which operate with minimal resources along with maximum efficiency.
